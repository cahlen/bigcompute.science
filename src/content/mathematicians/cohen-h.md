French mathematician who, with Lenstra, formulated the Cohen-Lenstra heuristics predicting the distribution of class groups of number fields. Our computation of 30 billion class numbers tests these predictions at a scale never before possible, revealing that convergence to the predicted 75% is far slower than expected.
