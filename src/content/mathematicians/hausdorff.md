German mathematician who introduced the concept of fractional dimension that bears his name. We compute dim_H(E_A) for over a million continued fraction Cantor sets. Hausdorff was persecuted under the Nazi regime; in 1942, rather than be sent to a concentration camp, he and his wife took their own lives.
