American mathematician whose 1901 classification of subgroups of SL₂(𝔽ₚ) we use to prove that the Zaremba semigroup acts transitively on (ℤ/pℤ)² for every prime p. This eliminates local obstructions to the conjecture — a result possible only because Dickson's century-old classification is complete.
