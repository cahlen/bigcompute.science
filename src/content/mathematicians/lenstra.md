Dutch mathematician who co-formulated the Cohen-Lenstra heuristics and co-invented the LLL lattice reduction algorithm. His probabilistic model for class groups — that groups appear with frequency proportional to 1/|Aut(G)| — is one of the most elegant conjectures in algebraic number theory.
