Indian mathematician who, largely self-taught, discovered thousands of formulas for mathematical constants — many involving continued fractions of extraordinary beauty. The Ramanujan Machine experiment is named for his approach: systematic discovery of formulas through pattern recognition, now accelerated by GPU computation.
